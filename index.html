NantHealth Accessibility Bookmark

Created to allow us to easily test any page quickly by clicking on a bookmark.

Install

Click and drag the link below to your address bar.

NH Accessibility Bookmarklet

Usage

Once your bookmark is installed

  1. Navigate to the page you want to test
  2. Click your bookmark to install the tester on the page
  3. Then click the accessibility icon which appears in the bottom right
  4. You should see the results appear on the right hand side of the page, NOTE for some large pages this may take some time.

Uninstall

Simply refresh your page and the page will load without the accessibility tester on it.
